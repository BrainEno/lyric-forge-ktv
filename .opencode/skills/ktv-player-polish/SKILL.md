## What I do

I help improve the playback layer of the KTV app.

## Primary concerns

- accompaniment playback stability
- lyric sync quality
- active line highlighting
- seek behavior
- global offset handling
- desktop and mobile usability
- Spotify-inspired playback feel

## UI expectations

- current content should feel central
- controls should feel tight and intentional
- lyric readability must remain high
- dark surfaces must preserve contrast
- progress and current position must be obvious

## Never do this

- do not make seek behavior desync lyrics silently
- do not hide playback errors
- do not bury offset adjustment
- do not make playback screens look like forms

## When to use me

Use this skill when:
- building or refining the player page
- implementing synced lyric scrolling
- adding seek / offset / replay behavior
- improving playback controls or now-playing layout
